Menemen Recipe

Menemen is a traditional Turkish dish made with eggs, tomatoes, peppers, and onions.

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 3 large tomato
  • 2 green pepper
  • 1 small onion
  • 2 tablespoons oil

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat.
  2. Add onions and saut√© until translucent.
  3. Add peppers and tomatoes, cook for 5 minutes.
  4. Crack eggs into the pan and stir gently until cooked through.
